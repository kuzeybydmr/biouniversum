How to Use BioUniversum

Welcome to BioUniversum, your AI-powered dashboard for exploring NASA's space biology publications. This guide will help you get started and understand most of the platform.

1. Searching for Articles

Use the search bar on the homepage to find articles by title or keyword. For example, type microgravity to view all studies related to that topic.

2. Viewing an Article

Click on a result to open its page. There, you will see:

  • Publication Year
  • Full Text: a direct link to the complete article on NCBI.
  • Keywords: clickable keywords that link to related results.
  • Abstract: the official summary provided in the publication.
  • AI Summary: a generated summary that condenses the full article for quicker understanding.

3. Exploring with Keywords

Keywords appear below the summary. Click on any keyword to instantly search for other articles related to that concept.

4. Finding the Best Match

Not sure where to start? Use the Find Matching Article feature to jump directly to the article that is most related to your search query.

5. Gallery

Visit the Gallery page to see inspiring photos from NASA's Space Biology experiments.

Tip: Summarization may take a few minutes, depending on the length of the article. Please allow the AI time to complete the process.
